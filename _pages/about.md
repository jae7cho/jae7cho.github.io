I am a 1st year Pyschology - Cognitive and Behavioral Neuroscience (CBN) PhD student advised by Dr. James C. Thompson at George Mason University (GMU).
I also graduated with a BS in Neuroscience at GMU where I studied the heritability of neurotransmitter mRNA expression and graph theoretical measures of network topology. During my clinical research at the Washington DC VA Medical Center, I focused on structural MRI, resting-state fMRI and ASL data to assess the impacts of exercise in veterans with schizophrenia. At the Child Mind Institute’s Center for the Developing Brain, I extended my work to test-retest reliability of fMRI analyses and the benchmarking of novel neural network models for brain extraction in non-human primates. As a Data Science Consultant at Accenture, I productionized predictive machine learning pipelines and applied advanced analytics to transform data into actionable insights for stakeholders. 

In my free time I am most likely to be found hiking, golfing or skiing - except on Sunday where I experience a spectrum of emotions watching the Philadelphia Eagles 🦅 Go Birds.
